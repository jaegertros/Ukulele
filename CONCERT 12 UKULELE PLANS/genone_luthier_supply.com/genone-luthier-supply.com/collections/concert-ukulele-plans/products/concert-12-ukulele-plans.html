Concert 12 Ukulele Plans

$18.95

  • Overview

  • General Information:

    Full sized, very detailed plans of a the intermediate-sized ukulele - the Concert sized model.

    This ukulele is a 12-fret version of the intermediate size of the ukulele family. The neck joins the body at the 12th fret. It is equipped with 4 strings, fan bracing for excellent sound production and great volume characteristics, and traditional surface mounted, classical guitar style, tie-on bridge, which additionally enhances the tone. Note that the bracing for the top plate does change significantly when compared to the 14-fret version of this instrument.
